About Greenfield University

Greenfield University, established in 1896, is committed to academic excellence and global leadership. Our campus welcomes students from over 60 countries and provides a rigorous, innovative environment for learning, discovery, and community service.

The university supports more than 20,000 undergraduate and graduate students, offering 120+ majors and fostering a culture of research, creativity, and inclusion.
